Our Donation Plan

Your support helps us continue our good work.

Food for a family

Food is a basic necessity for survival and also the key to a healthy life. Without regular healthy meals children struggle to go to school, carers struggle to work and communities are left battling to survive.

Shelter for a family

Without a proper home, it is very difficult or impossible for displaced families to find paid work. Many live in camps or informal settlements far from towns and markets, and can’t afford to travel.

Education for a child

Education is important for both men and women as both have an essential role in the development of a healthy and smart society. Education is an necessary way for delivering a brillianr future.

Their lives depend on your donation

Your gift of just $5, $10 or $20 will create the momentum needed to help the youth in our community get motivated to make a positive difference in their lives. We want to create a "snowball" effect through the help of our caring supporters to create a positive "avalanche of success" for everyone who benefits from our programs.
